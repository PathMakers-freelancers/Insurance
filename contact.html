Common Enquiries

Are enquiries kept confidential?

Absolutely. Every correspondence is handled under strict NDA protocols with end-to-end encryption.

Do you provide free valuations?

Yes. Our specialists offer complimentary preliminary estimates for items with an estimated value above £5,000.

How are items shipped after purchase?

We use bonded fine-art logistics partners with climate-controlled transit and full insurance for every consignment.

Can I participate from overseas?

Yes. AuctionNest supports live bidding from 80+ countries with real-time currency conversion and multi-language support.
