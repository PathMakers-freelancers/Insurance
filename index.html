Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become a registered collector on AuctionNest?

Register for a complimentary account, complete our identity verification process, and explore our live catalogue. Select any lot and tap 'Place Bid' to submit your offer or configure an automatic proxy ceiling.

What is the buyer's premium on successful bids?

A straightforward 5% buyer's premium is applied to every winning bid. This covers full provenance authentication, insured escrow settlement, and ongoing platform stewardship.

What shipping and delivery options are available?

Consignors arrange dispatch, and AuctionNest's vetted logistics partners provide optional fully insured white-glove delivery. Estimated transit times and costs are displayed on every lot page.

How does AuctionNest safeguard my payment information?

Every transaction is protected by enterprise-grade TLS encryption and routed through PCI-DSS-compliant gateways, including Stripe and PayPal, ensuring your financial data remains secure at all times.
