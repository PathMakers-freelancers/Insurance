Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place a bid?

Register for a free account, browse our live auctions, and click the "Place Bid" button on your desired item. You can enter a custom amount or use the quick bid options.

Is there a buyer's premium?

Yes, a standard buyer's premium of 5% applies to all winning bids to cover platform verification and processing services.

How is shipping handled?

Sellers are responsible for shipping. Shipping costs and estimated delivery times are listed on each individual auction page.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and partner with trusted payment gateways like Stripe and PayPal to ensure your data is always protected.
